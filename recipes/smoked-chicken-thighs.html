Smoked Chicken Thighs

Instructions for Delicious Smoked Chicken Thighs

Using this guide will help you to make perfectly cooked chicken thighs with a beautiful smokey flavor your family will love and your friends will crave!

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs fresh chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp Smokey Sweet BBQ Rub

Steps

  1. Preheat smoker to 230 degrees
  2. Coat chicken with a liberal amount of the BBQ rub
  3. Place on smoker and cook 3 hours or until temperature reaches 175 degrees
  4. Let meat rest 15 minutes before serving
