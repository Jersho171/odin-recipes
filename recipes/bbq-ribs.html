BBQ Ribs

Instructions for the Most Delicious Ribs

Follow these steps to make mouthwatering juicy ribs that will come out perfect every time!

Ingredients

  • Full rack of ribs
  • Sweet and Smokey BBQ Rub
  • Apple juice

Steps

  1. Preheat smoker to 180 degrees
  2. Generously apply rub to ribs
  3. Place ribs on smoker for 3 hours
  4. Take ribs out and cover in aluminum foil, then pour 1.5 cups of apple juice over ribs in foil, place back on smoker for 2 hours at 225
  5. Take ribs out of foil and let smoke for another 1 hour or until temp reaches 205 degrees
